Benjamin Kores is a Computer Science Major at the College of Staten Island in Staten Island, NY and graduated from Staten Island Technical High School in 2019. He discovered his love of cooking at the age of 7, when he helped his mother bake chocolate chip cookies for the very first time. Ever since then, he cooked and baked whenever he could, challenging himself to do more and more without the help of his mother, who is an excellent chef herself, and to come up with more and more innovative and delicious recipes. Ben's other hobbies include swimming, coding, theater, and watching movies and TV. He was on the Trident Aquatics Club Swim Team at the JCC in Staten Island for over 10 years and was on the Staten Island Tech swim team, and won multiple gold medals at swim meets. Ben's love of coding manifested when he got his first Android tablet at the age of 12. He was fascinated by how the software worked and was obssessed with figuring it out. Now he is proficient in Java, Python, and MySQL Server, and hopes to be a software developer at a major tech company like Google, preferably for mobile app development. Ben is also a huge theater nerd. He loves to act and sing and performed in many middle school and high school plays. If his software career doesn't pan out, he hopes to enter the theater industry. Ben is also a huge Marvel and DC fan and loves to watch comic book movies and TV shows whenever he gets the chance.
